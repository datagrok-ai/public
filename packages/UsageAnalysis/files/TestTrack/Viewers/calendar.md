### Calendar viewer

1. Open the **demog** dataset.
2. Click the **Add viewer** icon, find **Calendar** viewer and press on it. **Calendar** viewer opens
3. Close previously opened viewer. On the **Viewers tab**, click **Calendar** icon. **Calendar** viewer opens
4. Interact with the viewer:
   * Hover over a day cell, a month label (left column), and a day-of-week header (S/M/T/W/T/F/S) — a tooltip appears showing the matching rows with a "Click to select" hint; hovered day number becomes bold
   * Click a day cell — rows for that date get selected (visible as orange inner circles and in the row count)
   * Click a month label — all rows in that month get selected
   * Click a day-of-week header — all rows for that weekday get selected
   * Shift+click another region — selection is extended; Ctrl+click — toggled
5. Hover on the **Calendar** viewer, click the **Gear** icon. The **Property Pane** opens.
6. Modify various properties in **Property Pane**:
* Expected Result: Changes to the properties should be reflected in the Calendar viewer without errors, and the viewer should be updated accordingly.
