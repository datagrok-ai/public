### Grid viewer

1. Open SPGI and SPGI-linked1 datasets
1. Go to SPGI, click the **Add viewer** icon, and select **Grid**
1. Close the viewer
1. On the **Toolbox > Viewers** tab, click the **Grid** icon to add it again
1. Interact with cells, headers, selection, and scrollbars directly on the viewer
1. Turn on color coding for a numeric column and verify it renders
1. With the Grid viewer selected, open the **Context Panel**
1. Go to **Data > Table** and switch to **SPGI-linked1** - **viewer re-binds to the new table**
1. Modify other settings (row height, fonts, frozen columns) - **changes are reflected in the viewer**
1. Drag the column header bottom border to change header height; drag a row number border to change row height
1. Save the layout
1. Change the layout (e.g. add some viewers)
1. Apply the saved layout - **verify that all settings, including visual adjustments, are restored**
1. Save the project
1. Close All
1. Open the project
