# Microscopic Findings

Cross-domain analysis linking microscopic findings with laboratory data.

Key features:
- **Laboratory-histopathology relationship**: visualizes how laboratory value distributions relate to microscopic findings and their severity, helping identify biomarker signals associated with pathological changes
- **Summary frequency table**: provides a frequency table for all microscopic findings across the study

The view allows exploration of pathological findings across treatment arms to identify treatment-related effects and correlate them with laboratory biomarker changes.
