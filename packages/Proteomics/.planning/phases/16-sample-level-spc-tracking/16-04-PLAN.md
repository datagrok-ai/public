<objective>
Extend the Spectronaut PG-report parser (BOTH streaming and text paths) to capture `R.RunDate` (earliest observed) and `R.InstrumentMethod` (first observed) when present in the header, and stash the captured pair on the parsed df as the `proteomics.spc_run_meta_seed` tag. This seed feeds Plan 05's Annotate Experiment dialog, which prefills the two new inputs (instrument_id + acquisition_datetime) so Cytokinetics operators rarely have to type either value.

Purpose: Without parser-side seeding, every Spectronaut import requires the operator to read the run sheet and re-type the instrument + date into the dialog. With it, the dialog opens with both fields pre-populated and the operator just confirms. This is the single load-bearing UX simplification in Phase 16 for the most-used parser.

Output: `src/parsers/spectronaut-parser.ts` edited in two places (streaming column-index resolution at lines 398-406 and the corresponding text-path resolution earlier in the file). One new RED test `SPC:spectronaut_seed` turns GREEN.
</objective>
